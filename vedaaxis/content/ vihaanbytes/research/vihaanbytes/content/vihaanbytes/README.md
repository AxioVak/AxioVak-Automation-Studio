# VihaanBytes - Research & Development Hub

VihaanBytes is a research and development sub-project supporting vedaaxis and other AxioVak initiatives.

## 🎯 Purpose

- **Research Organization** - Compile and structure research from multiple sources
- **Content Development** - Transform research into publishable content
- **Knowledge Management** - Centralize learning and insights
- **Integration Hub** - Bridge between NotebookLM research and final outputs

## 📁 Folder Structure

### `research/`
Raw and organized research materials:
- Extracted notes from NotebookLM
- Source material compilations
- Research summaries and syntheses
- Reference documents

### `content/`
Processed, publication-ready materials:
- Blog posts and articles
- Educational guides
- Content frameworks
- Final deliverables

---

## 🔄 Workflow

1. **Source Research** - Gather information in NotebookLM
2. **Export & Organize** - Move research files to `research/` folder
3. **Process Content** - Transform research into usable content
4. **Finalize** - Move completed work to `content/` folder
5. **Distribute** - Publish to appropriate channels

---

## 🔗 Integration Points

- **Upstream:** NotebookLM (research generation)
- **Upstream:** vedaaxis (content consumption)
