# Core Vedic & Psychological Mappings

## Foundation Concepts
- **Manas (Lower Mind) & Buddhi (Intellect):** Mental discipline & attention control
- **Prana (Vital Energy):** Channeling power toward meaningful goals
- **Vidya & Vac (Knowledge & Sound):** Knowledge through rhythm & practice
- **Vairagya (Emotional Balance):** Clarity & non-attachment
- **Sadhana (Discipline):** Razor-sharp focus
- **Sandhi (Sacred Junctions):** Fluid logic & creative problem-solving
- **Sthita-prajna (Steady Wisdom):** Integrity & moral alignment
- **Maya (Illusion):** Distinguishing real value from illusion
- **Pancha Bhootas (Five Elements):** Earth (Routine), Water (Flow), Fire (Transformation), Air (Breath), Space (Vision)
- **Swargarohana (Final Ascent):** Liberation through releasing attachment
