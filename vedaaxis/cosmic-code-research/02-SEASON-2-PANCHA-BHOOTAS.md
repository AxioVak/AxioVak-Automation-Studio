# Season 2: The Pancha Bhootas (Five Elements)

**Theme:** Five Elements mapped to mastery of daily habits and physical foundations
**Episodes:** 5
**Arc:** Rock-solid daily base (Earth) to grand life goals (Space)

## Episodes
- E01: Prithvi (Earth) - Building Routine & Foundation
- E02: Jala (Water) - Stewardship of Time & Habits
- E03: Agni (Fire) - Forging Resilience Through Challenge
- E04: Vayu (Air) - Finding Harmony & Deep Breathing
- E05: Akasha (Space) - Goal Setting & Vision
