# AI Prompts Reference Guide

## Clean Core Visual Governance Rule
ZERO modern technology artifacts, digital screens, sci-fi code, or holograms permitted.
Visuals MUST be: Purely organic, tactile, historically grounded.

## Kling AI Prompt Wrapper
A high-end cinematic 4K video shot, uniform ancient Indian art aesthetic, heavily textured weathered stone and warm terracotta relief sculpture, raw chiseled surfaces, photorealistic rock textures, dramatic side-lighting, deep shadows. [INSERT SCENE ACTION], rendered in natural ochre, warm terracotta, sand-carved stone tones. Minimalist color palette, organic textures. Smooth cinematic camera movement.

NO: extra fingers, mutated hands, six fingers, extra limbs, distorted anatomy, vibrant neons, modern digital gradients, saturated plastics.
