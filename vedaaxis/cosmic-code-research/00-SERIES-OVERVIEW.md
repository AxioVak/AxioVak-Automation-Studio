# The Cosmic Code - Mini Series

**Project Name:** The Cosmic Code - Mini Series (VedaAxis Channel Architecture)
**Target Audience:** Kids & Families (Strictly PG, Engaging, and Accessible)
**Format:** 4-Minute Episodes

## Overview
Decoding ancient architectural blueprints for the next generation of builders. The series bridges ancient Vedic psychological blueprints with modern children's everyday behaviors, grounded firmly in the academic history and engineering of the Indus Valley Civilisation (IVC).

## The "Crawl-Walk-Run" Framework
- **Crawl:** Real-world Indus Valley archaeological evidence
- **Walk:** Vedic/Epic narrative and psychological principle  
- **Run:** Direct application to modern child behavior

**Total Seasons:** 4 | **Total Episodes:** 23+ | **Episode Duration:** ~4 minutes
